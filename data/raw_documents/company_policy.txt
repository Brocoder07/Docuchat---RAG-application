Company Vacation Policy

All full-time employees are entitled to 15 vacation days per year.
Vacation days accrue at a rate of 1.25 days per month.
Employees can carry over up to 5 unused vacation days to the next year.
Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.

Health Insurance

The company offers comprehensive health insurance through Blue Cross.
Dental and vision coverage are included in the basic plan.
The company covers 80% of premium costs for employees.
New employees are eligible for insurance after 30 days of employment.

Work From Home Policy

Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
Remote work equipment will be provided by the company.
All employees must attend monthly in-person team meetings.
